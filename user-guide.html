Introduction

Welcome to Constellation, your all-in-one platform for intelligent sales and customer relationship management. This guide will walk you through the core features of the application to help you streamline your workflow and close more deals.

Part 1: Overview

This section provides a high-level understanding of each major module within Constellation, outlining their purpose and key features.

1. The Command Center: Your Daily Hub

The Command Center is your home base. It's the first page you see after logging in and is designed to show you exactly what you need to focus on for the day, from manual tasks to automated sequence steps.

Key Features:

  • My Tasks: This table lists all tasks you've manually created. Tasks that are past their due date are highlighted in red so you can prioritize them.
  • Add New Task Button: Quickly create new tasks and link them to contacts or accounts.
  • Sequence Steps Due: Your automated to-do list, showing sequence steps that are due today or are overdue.
  • Actionable Steps: Dedicated buttons for streamlining sequence steps. For email steps, clicking "Send Email" now opens a modal where you can review and edit the message before it's sent to your default email client. All lookups like [FirstName] and [AccountName] are automatically populated.
  • Upcoming Sequence Steps: A forward-looking view of automated outreach, helping you prepare for future engagements.
  • Recent Activities: A live feed of your latest logged activities across all contacts and accounts.

2. Deals: Managing Your Pipeline

The Deals page provides a visual, Kanban-style board to manage your sales pipeline. You can drag and drop deals from one stage to the next, helping you track progress and identify bottlenecks.

Key Features:

  • Customizable Pipeline: Track deals across stages like "Prospecting," "Qualification," "Proposal," and "Closed Won/Lost."
  • At-a-Glance Analytics: The top of the page displays key metrics, including your total pipeline value, win rate, and average deal size.
  • Deal-Specific Information: Click on any deal card to view detailed information, log activities, or create associated tasks.

3. Contacts & Accounts: Your Core Database

These sections are the heart of your CRM, allowing you to manage all the people and companies you do business with.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive Profiles: Every contact and account has a detailed profile page showing all associated tasks, activities, deals, and sequences.
  • Bulk Import: Use the provided CSV templates to quickly import existing lists of contacts and accounts, saving you hours of manual data entry.
  • Advanced Filtering: Easily search and filter your lists to find exactly who you're looking for.

4. Sequences: Automating Your Outreach

Sequences allow you to build automated outreach campaigns with multiple steps, ensuring consistent and timely follow-up with prospects.

Key Features:

  • Multi-Step Campaigns: Create sequences with a mix of automated emails, manual tasks, and LinkedIn connection requests.
  • Custom Delays: Set the number of days to wait between each step to ensure your outreach feels natural and not robotic.
  • Personalization with Lookups: Use placeholders like [FirstName] and [AccountName] in your email templates, which will be automatically replaced with the contact's information.

5. Cognito: AI-Powered Intelligence

Cognito is your built-in AI assistant, designed to scan for market intelligence and help you craft the perfect outreach message.

Key Features:

  • Intelligence Alerts: Cognito automatically surfaces important buying signals related to your accounts, such as executive hires, funding rounds, or company expansions.
  • AI Email Drafting: On any alert, click the "Action" button to have Cognito's AI draft a personalized outreach email based on the specific signal. You can then refine the draft with your own prompts before sending.

6. Social Hub: Amplify Your Voice

The Social Hub is your content center for building your professional brand and engaging with your network.

Key Features:

  • Curated Content: The hub provides a mix of AI-curated news articles relevant to your industry and pre-approved marketing assets from your team.
  • Easy Sharing: Quickly share campaign assets and relevant news to your professional networks like LinkedIn.

Part 2: How-To Guides

How-To: Execute a Sequence Email Step

  1. Navigate to the Command Center.
  2. In the "Sequence Steps Due" table, locate an email task and click the "Send Email" button.
  3. A modal window will appear with the subject and body of the email. All lookups (e.g., [FirstName]) will be automatically filled in.
  4. Review the email and make any desired edits directly in the modal.
  5. Click "Send with Email Client". This will open your computer's default email application with the message ready to send.
  6. After you send the email from your client, the activity will be automatically logged in Constellation, and the contact will advance to the next step in the sequence.

How-To: Use Cognito Intelligence Alerts

  1. Navigate to the Cognito page.
  2. Review the Alert Cards, which provide summaries of important buying signals.
  3. On a relevant alert, click the "Action" button.
  4. The Action Center modal will open, where Cognito's AI will have drafted a personalized email.
  5. Review the draft. Use the "Refine with Custom Prompt" input to ask the AI to make changes (e.g., "Make it shorter and more casual").
  6. Once satisfied, copy the text and send it via your email client. You can also create follow-up tasks directly from the modal.
