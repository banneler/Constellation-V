Part 2: How-To Guides

How-To: Execute a Sequence Email Step

  1. Navigate to the Command Center.
  2. In the "Sequence Steps Due" table, click the "Send Email" button.
  3. A modal will appear with the pre-filled email.
  4. Click "Send with Email Client".
  5. The activity is logged, and the sequence advances.

How-To: Use Cognito Intelligence Alerts

  1. Navigate to the Cognito page.
  2. On an alert, click the "Action" button.
  3. Review and refine the AI-drafted email.
  4. Copy the text to send from your email client.
